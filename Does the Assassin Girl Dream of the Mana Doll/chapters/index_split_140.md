## Chapter 163: Curtain Call

The air trembled.

Sound, wind, explosion.

And the ground shattered.

But no one was there.

The woman he aimed for had already widened the distance.

He charged straight toward her, drawn by her figure, but a moment after
he stepped in, she flowed sideways at a timing that made it impossible
for him to change direction, keeping out of his sight.

The massive body that generated fearsome power also created harsh
inertia.

His body, his arm, was far too heavy to stop the descending punch.

In the moment the fist uselessly struck the ground, pain shot through
the arm that swung and the foot that stepped in. No, it *should’ve* been
painful. But he no longer registered it as pain.

Letty sighed softly as she observed his unflinching movements.

"...I knew it, but you stopped being human a long time ago."

She couldn't help but feel a slight pity. The fact that he was remade
into an undead being meant that there was a power that created him. That
is, he had been reduced to a mere tool.

The armored figure from a little while ago was still marginally better.
Now that it had come to this, he could no longer be called a being with
a will.

This was a path she herself might have followed, if things had gone
wrong. If Greg had been just a little more self-serving. If she hadn't
accepted that request that day.

Most of all, if she hadn't won back then.

The one who would have been reduced to a miserably disposable tool would
surely have been her.

So, she felt a little sorry for him. Even if he brought it upon himself.

"WHY!!! WHY!!!"

He roared an eternal, unanswerable question. The one asking it no longer
had the ears to hear the answer.

If that was the case, what she needed to provide was—

Her thought process was interrupted by an incoming horizontal arm sweep.

Although his arm was long, it was still just an arm. By keeping her
distance, she could slip beneath it.

And then, she stepped into the opening created by the evasion. Just as
she thought it was her turn, she saw him forcibly stop and raise his
foot.

Accompanied by the sound of something snapping.

His raised foot, which let out a dull sound from his pivot leg, was
brought down.

But a kick that required two distinct movements could never land.

She accurately read the descending foot, evaded it at the very last
moment, and changed her evasive motion into a rotation. She delivered a
fierce horizontal slash, and... the bottom of his leg, from the ankle
down, was severed.

"WHY!!! STOP MESSING AROUND!!!"

It was a scream that was meaningful yet meaningless. He was merely
spitting out the words ingrained in his soul like a curse.

...If those were the only words ingrained in him, what kind of life had
he led?

Although she was the primary reason for his current state.

"Why, indeed? I hope you can think about that next time."

*Will there be a next time for him?* Letty, who had no religious faith,
had no idea. But she knew that she had to put an end to this.

*Squish, squish*.

Making dull sounds of crushed flesh and trampled blood, he braced
himself.

He probably felt no pain. He merely stepped on the ground with the
severed cross-section of his leg.

Twisting his body violently with a nasty sound, he turned towards Letty.

The only expression on his face and in his eyes was pure malice and
resentment.

Driven by that, he tried to step forward forcefully and... collapsed
onto his knees.

Supporting his weight required a fully functional ankle. Therefore, the
rotting flesh no longer had the strength to hold him up.

He was the only one unaware of this self-evident fact as he clawed his
way toward her. His collapsed balance was forcibly recovered by raw
muscle power, only to collapse again, and then recovered, only to
collapse again.

"I never realized. You were so completely lacking..."

The movements that looked supple. The movements that seemed to defy
gravity.

All of it was merely due to his exceptional physique. That was all it
was.

Sadly, that fact was now glaringly exposed.

Even though he was a shadow of his former self, she remembered. His body
movements were indeed like this.

"A tiger in a cage, huh..."

Should she say 'As expected,' or 'How sad'?

It seemed Dominique's words were right on the mark.

He lurched toward her. He raised his arm. He brought it down.

Everything was both bizarrely fast and bizarrely slow.

She could see it. She could move. She could evade it. Not only that, but
she could counterattack.

It was tragically transparent.

And once again, her blade flashed.

Under the continuous slashes, his left arm finally gave way and was
severed.

Losing his balance, he collapsed heavily to the ground.

But he sprang up immediately, glaring at Letty. As if to say, “This
isn't over.”

"STOP MESSING AROUND!! STOP MESSING AROUND!! I AM, I AMMMMM!!"

*Is there still a small part of him left?*

Though even that seemed to be a flickering flame.

His fist clenched with an audible *squelch*. He raised it nevertheless.

She had no plans to be careless. No matter how visible his move was.

The monster he had become could do anything.

She seemed to be looking at the raised arm, but she wasn't.

She was looking at his entire being, making sure not to miss whatever he
was planning to do.

And as he brought the fist down, she flowed sideways again.

The fist sliced through the air uselessly, only smashing the ground.

And then,

A dull, resounding thud.

A focused strike to his flank, where he was completely off balance. A
strike aimed at the core, which was emitting magical power.

"...As expected, it's hard."

The hardness was incomparable to the demon core she had crushed before.
The density of the condensed mana must be on a different level.

If that’s the case…

"I still have ways to deal with this..."

Saying that, she flowed sideways again.

She gracefully avoided the incoming open palm, likely an attempt to
cover a wider area.

And then, a full-power horizontal slash at the arm he had just brought
down.

She braced her foot on the ground, channeling the force of her step, the
power of her legs, her hips, her back, and her core.

The resulting power, converted into rotation along her body's axis...
effortlessly sliced off his arm.

*Squelch*, he collapsed to the ground with a sickening sound.

She glided next to his squirming form.

"...Goodbye."

And once more.

She pushed off the ground. She stepped into the close distance.

She received the momentum of her step by bracing her heel firmly.

A feeling of unity with the ground, with the earth.

"Oh, my. She grasped it, didn't she?" Dominique, who had been observing,
murmured. She sounded genuinely delighted, with only a hint of regret.
She herself had only reached that state when she was a little older than
Letty.

"Well, she must have had a good master," she quipped.

Letty's body control was clearly on a different level.

The force of her step, her own body weight, the hardness of the earth,
even gravity.

She converted all of it into her own power, transmitting it without loss
to her back and arm.

And then, it was delivered.

The ultimate precision.

The power born from accumulated training, channeled by accumulated
technique, was accurately thrust in without the slightest tremor, as if
sucked in.

A faint, slight *thud*.

And the core was pierced.

Immediately after, it shattered, and the stored mana was violently
released.

And after the blast cleared,

There remained the scattered remnants of Curtis.

And, standing quietly, the figure of Letty.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

What is shattered is his body. And his conspiracy.

The means he employed were thwarted, and he could no longer find refuge
anywhere.

And a final, desperate struggle. Even though he didn't know whether it
would make any difference to his opponent.
